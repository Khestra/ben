3. Artist’s Note

This remix project represents the power of storytelling through motion. Using fast-paced editing and rhythmic flow, I blended multiple scenes to express the complexity of urban emotions in a digital world.

I intentionally layered scenes with lo-fi sound to create emotional tension and narrative contrast. Every visual choice was a reflection of balance between chaos and calm, and the remix process helped me realize how deeply editing can shape perception.
